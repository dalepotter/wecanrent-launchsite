What do we do?

How we make landlords lives easier

We’re different to all other high street and online letting agents.

We check tenant references and credit reports before they’re invited to view your property. This means you can prioritise the most suitable tenants for viewings to avoid wasting time.

When potential tenants attend the viewing, you’ll know they are ‘ready to rent’! Once you’ve chosen your perfect tenant, you can sign them up right away. This eliminates the time and cost wasted on enthusiastic, yet unsuitable renters.

How it works

How it works for landlords:

1. Upload your property

Upload your property details and choose your tenant preferences. We advertise your property on all the major portals including Rightmove and Zoopla.

2. Get pre-referenced tenants

We speak to interested tenants and complete checks to ensure their suitability and affordability. We connect you with the best tenants.

3. Pick your perfect tenant

Show the property to your top tenants and pick the tenants you want. The tenant checks are already completed, so use our simple online platform to confirm the paperwork without delay.
